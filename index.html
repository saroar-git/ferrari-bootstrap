CREATE YOUR OWN FERRARI

EVERY FERRARI IS UNIQUE. MAKE YOUR MODEL UNIQUELY YOURS

In the early days, Ferrari clients had huge freedom of choice in terms of fabrics, leathers, woods, colours and finishes, resulting in some genuinely one-off creations tailored minutely to individual tastes and desires. Today Ferrari maintains this tradition, offering a range of programmes that enable every last detail of clients’ cars to be tailored according to their personal taste using the Configurator Tool, before selecting one of our special customisation programmes to meet their individual requirements.

ATELIER FERRARI

MAKE YOUR MODEL UNIQUELY YOURS

Atelier Ferrari is a special studio for clients to visit to make their car even more unique, and mould it to their personal tastes. The Atelier is a sophisticated environment with a wide selection of materials and accessories available to see and feel. It is designed to ensure the client is the centre of attention, providing all the inspiration they need to create their very own bespoke car assisted by our team of experts.
